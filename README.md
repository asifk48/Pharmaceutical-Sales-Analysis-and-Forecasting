# 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a complete **Pharmaceutical Sales Analysis Dashboard** developed using **Power BI** and **Python (Google Colab)**.

The project focuses on analyzing pharmaceutical sales performance, identifying business trends, and forecasting future sales using Machine Learning techniques.

The dashboard helps businesses monitor sales performance, identify high-performing products, analyze growth trends, and understand future sales direction.

---

# ❓ Business Questions Solved

- What is the total sales revenue for the given period?
- What is the sales growth percentage compared to the previous period?
- Which products are the top sellers in terms of revenue?
- Which products are the best-selling in terms of quantity?
- What are the sales trends over time (daily, monthly, quarterly)?
- Are there any consistent peaks or drops in sales?
- What are the projected future sales trends?

---

# 🚀 Dashboard Highlights

## 1️⃣ Sales Performance Dashboard 💰

This dashboard provides detailed insights into overall sales performance and product contribution.

### Key Insights

- **Sales Growth Analysis:** Month-over-month growth percentage was analyzed to identify business expansion and decline periods.

- **Top Revenue Generating Products:** Products contributing the highest revenue were identified using Brand Name analysis.

- **Best Selling Products by Quantity:** High-volume products were analyzed to understand customer demand patterns.

- **Revenue Tracking:** KPI cards were used to monitor total sales revenue and business growth.

---

## 2️⃣ Sales Trend Analysis 📈

This section focuses on analyzing sales behavior over different time periods.

### Key Insights

- **Monthly Analysis:** Monthly sales trends helped identify high-performing and low-performing months.

- **Quarterly Analysis:** Quarterly performance analysis revealed periods of business growth and decline.

- **Peak and Drop Detection:** Certain periods showed sales peaks while some months experienced drops due to changing product demand.

---

## 3️⃣ Sales Forecasting using Machine Learning 🤖

Future sales forecasting was performed using **Python in Google Colab** with a **Linear Regression model**.

### Forecasting Highlights

- Historical monthly sales data was used for forecasting future sales.

- A Machine Learning model was trained to identify overall sales trends.

- The model predicted sales for future periods.

- Forecasted sales were visualized along with historical sales data.
